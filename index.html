Our Story

BookBase started with a simple idea: that reading shouldn't be a solitary activity. We wanted to create a space where book lovers could not only track their collections but also share their passion with others.

Today, we're proud to support a global community of readers, collectors, and bibliophiles who are reimagining the traditional library for the modern age.

Our Mission

To connect the world through the power of shared stories and physical book local lending.

Our Vision

To become the world's largest distributed library, powered by readers like you.
